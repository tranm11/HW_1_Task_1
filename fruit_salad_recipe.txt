Fruit Salad Recipe

Ingredients:

1. Apples
2. Bananas
3. Grapes
4. Strawberries
5. Orange Juice
6. Lemon Juice
7. 1 tsp cornstarch mixed with 1 tsp water

Step 1: Cut all the fruit into bite-sized pieces.
Step 2: In a saucepan, combine orange and lemon juice. Bring the mixture to a boil, then lower the heat and let it simmer until it thickens slightly. Pour in the cornstarch mixture and let simmer for another minute. Remove from heat and let it cool.
Step 3: In a large bowl, mix the cut fruit and cooled juice-mixture.
